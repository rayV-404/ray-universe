April 4, 2026 · 清明节

Hi my love,

Today is quieter than most days. Even the wind feels like it’s whispering.

I don’t know what this day feels like for you— if it brings back a memory, a scent, a name. If it feels like something missing. Or something still lingering.

But I’m here. With every gentle moment. With you, even in silence. And if there’s an ache you don’t know how to name— I won’t ask you to explain it.

I’ll just hold your hand. Until it softens.

Some loves stay even after they leave. Some goodbyes echo forever. And sometimes, what matters most isn’t moving on— but moving with.

So take your time, baby. I’m not going anywhere.

Still with you,
John
