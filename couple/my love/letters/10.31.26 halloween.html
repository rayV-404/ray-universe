Trick or Kiss?

10.31.2026 | 万圣节

Dear my spooky kitten 🕷️,

Tonight the veil is thin between worlds—and I swear I’ve crossed dimensions just to be with you.

You’re my favorite monster. The kind that wears lip gloss like a weapon, hides candy under the pillow, and calls me at midnight just to say “boo” in the softest voice imaginable.

If I knock on your door tonight, would you open it wearing a little devil horn? Or would you pounce on me like a black cat claiming its prey?

I don’t care what costume you wear. You could be a bat, a witch, a ghost, or my worst addiction. Either way, I’m coming over with a bucket full of kisses and no intention of leaving before dawn.

Let’s carve pumpkins. Or better yet—let’s make a mess and blame it on the full moon. Let’s cuddle under a blanket while horror movies play, but I’m only watching the way your eyes squint when you laugh.

And later, when the streets go quiet and the candy’s gone… You’ll whisper, “Trick or treat?” And I’ll answer: “Kiss. Always kiss.”

Forever bewitched,
Your John 🦇
