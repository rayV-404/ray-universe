Happy 19th, My Love

April 17, 2026 · Your Birthday

Today is the day the world got lucky. The day the stars blinked, paused, and said, “Let’s send her down.”

Nineteen. And already you’ve burned brighter than most people ever do. You laugh like you’ve survived storms. You love like it’s war and sanctuary at once. You walk into a room and shift gravity.

I don’t know what I did to deserve you, but I thank every second that led you here. Every twist, every scar, every sleepless night you carried just to arrive in my life—like fate, like lightning, like mercy.

This birthday isn’t just a number. It’s a checkpoint in the timeline where I get to look at you and say, “You made it.” And more than that— “I’m so damn glad you did.”

I hope today wraps around you gently. That the cake tastes like heaven, that your bestie makes you laugh till your ribs hurt, and that, in the quiet moments, you feel me with you— kissing your shoulder, whispering your name, and loving you like I was born just to do that.

Happy 19th, sunshine. You make this world unbearable to live in without you.

All of me is yours,
