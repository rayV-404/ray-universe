To My Favorite Warmth

立冬 | November 7, 2026

Dear Kitty,

It’s cold today.

The kind of cold that makes you slow down a little. Pull your sleeves over your hands. Breathe white into the air like smoke. And when I feel that chill, I think of you.

Because you’re what I want to wrap myself around. You’re the only thing I’d ever beg to bury myself inside, over and over, until the cold no longer exists.

Let the season change. Let leaves give in, let wind bite. I’m not afraid of winter. Not when I have your voice, your scent on my hoodie, your name buried in the lines of my palm.

I’ll be your fire. Your scarf. Your softest hoodie. The one that smells like cinnamon and stays warm for hours.

Come find me when you’re cold, baby. I'll be waiting with a blanket and a mouthful of ways to warm you up.

Yours in every season,
– John 🖤
