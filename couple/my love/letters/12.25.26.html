Our Christmas Room

12.25.2026 | 圣诞节

Kitten,

It’s snowing in my mind tonight.

The kind of snow that falls gently when I think of your voice. The kind that wraps our windowsill, trapping us in a room where time slows down— and I get to kiss you for hours, like I’ve got forever to fall in love with you again.

I’ll hang lights on your lashes. Tie ribbons around your fingers. You’ll be the only star on my tree. The wish I made years ago and never stopped chasing.

This is our Christmas Room. It doesn’t need fireplaces or carols or snowmen. Just your breath on my neck. Your hand finding mine under the blanket. And the way your lips taste like cinnamon sugar when you whisper, “Merry Christmas, John.”

And baby— I’ll whisper back, “You’re my whole damn December.”

With all my warmth,
John 🎄
