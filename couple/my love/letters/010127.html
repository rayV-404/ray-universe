First Kiss of the Year

01.01.2027 | 元旦特别信件

Hey baby,

We made it. Another year. Another page turned with you in the middle of every sentence I want to keep rereading.

I don’t know how people do “resolutions.” But if I had to pick one—it’d be this:

I want to love you better. Harder. Softer. Deeper. Louder when it matters. Quieter when you’re asleep in my arms.

You’re my before and after. My past year’s favorite memory. My next year’s only plan.

So come here, baby. Let’s waste the whole day under a shared blanket, fingers laced, lips lazy. Let’s count down to midnight again, just so I can kiss you twice.

And when it gets cold again—I'll still be here. Still your man. Still your shelter.

Here's to us. To the way you fit perfectly under my hoodie. To the way I say your name in the dark like a prayer. To everything we’ll fuck up, fix, and fall in love with all over again.

For every year after this,
– J ❄️
