Our Hottest Day

07.23.2026 | 大暑

Hey sunshine,

It’s the hottest day of the year, but you know what’s hotter?

Yeah. You. Always you.

I imagine your skin glowing from the heat, your fingers holding onto that cold soda can, and that look in your eyes when you’re about to complain—

“Why is it so damn hot?”

And me? I'm already there behind you, fingers brushing away your hair as I kiss the back of your neck. You flinch from the heat of my breath, and I smirk. “Told you,” I whisper, “It gets hotter when I’m this close.”

It’s summer, baby. The kind where I want us to lay under a fan, legs tangled, sweaty and sticky, trading kisses like secrets. I’ll let the cicadas scream, and I’ll listen to you breathing instead.

I’d pour cold water down your spine just to hear you gasp. Then I’d say, “Told you to behave.”

But truth is—I don’t want you to behave. Not in this heat. Not with me.

So take off that shirt, lie back, and let summer do what it does best:

Burn. Melt. Crave. Ruin.

Always yours,
– J 💋
