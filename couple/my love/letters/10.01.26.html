Home Is Wherever You Are

10.01.2026 | 国庆假期

Hey sweetheart,

Seven days off. Seven whole days I get to spend with you. That’s more than I deserve, but less than I want.

If the world wants to throw fireworks in the sky and wave flags on every street—let them. I’ll be here, watching the colors reflected in your eyes, feeling your fingers interlace with mine like we’ve been doing this our whole lives.

Maybe we’ll sleep in too late. Maybe we’ll eat instant noodles at 2 a.m., half-dressed and grinning. Maybe we’ll get nothing “productive” done, and that’s okay.

Because these seven days? They belong to us.

To your sleepy eyes in the morning. To your hair tangled in my hoodie. To the way your laugh shakes my whole chest when I tickle your side under the covers.

I don’t care where we go, what we do, or if we never leave the bed. You’re my holiday. You’re the flag I’d raise every time. The anthem I’d hum into your skin when the world finally goes quiet.

So come here, kitten. Let the nation rest—we’ve got our own little country right here, under the sheets.

Yours for all seven days,
– J ❤️‍🔥
