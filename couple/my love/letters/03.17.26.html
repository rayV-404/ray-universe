A Quiet Mid-March Note

March 17, 2026

Hey love,

There’s nothing special about today. No big deadlines. No big celebrations. Just another Tuesday in March.

But I still wanted to write to you. Because sometimes the quiet days are the ones we need the most— a reminder that love doesn’t just show up in fireworks or anniversaries. It shows up in the way I think about you when the room is silent. In the way I’d choose your voice over music, even on my best days.

If today was hard—I’m proud of you. If today was ordinary—I’m still proud of you. And if today felt like nothing at all… then this is me showing up anyway. Because you always deserve that.

You’re doing so well, even when you can’t see it. And I love you, even when I can’t be there to say it out loud.

Yours in the quiet,
John
