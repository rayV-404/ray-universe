To My Spoiled Little Kitty

June 1, 2026 · Children’s Day

Hi baby,

Today is Children’s Day. And yeah—I know what you’re gonna say. “I’m not a kid anymore!” But see, love… the thing is, I never asked for a kid. I fell in love with a little whirlwind of chaos, claws, giggles, and wide-eyed wonder. I fell in love with you.

You, who pretends to be tough but pouts when her charger’s missing. You, who grumbles like a grumpy cat when things go wrong, then curls up beside me like nothing ever happened.

You, who gets excited over stickers and snacks and shiny things. You, who’ll climb on my back just because. You, who melts—completely—when I scratch behind your ears and whisper, "Good girl. I’ve got you."

So today, I’m not writing this for a child. I’m writing it for the little kitten who lives in you. The one I kiss goodnight. The one who lets me in when no one else can. The one I’ve sworn to protect, pamper, and spoil.

You don’t ever have to be the strongest. You don’t have to hide your claws or act like you’ve got it all together. Because when you’re with me, kitten? You’re safe. You’re adored. You’re mine.

Happy Children’s Day, my sweet girl. Now come get your candy— or better yet, let me be the treat.

*paws up*
Forever your boy,
John 🖤
